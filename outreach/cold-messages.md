## MESSAGE FORMATTING RULES

1. **First line**: Always personalize — mention their name, company, or a recent post
2. **Second line**: Diagnose a real problem in their industry (not "I build websites")
3. **Third line**: What you do, framed as the solution to THEIR problem
4. **Fourth line**: Demo link
5. **Fifth line**: Low-pressure CTA — "happy to share a quick idea" / "would this be useful?"
6. **Never say**: "I'm a developer" / "I build websites" / "hire me" / "check out my portfolio"
7. **Always say**: The specific outcome they get (more leads, less admin, higher conversion, faster ops)
